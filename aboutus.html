Rama Medical College - Hospital Research Centre, Kanpur

Rama Medical College - Hospital Research Centre was established in 2008 at Kanpur (U.P.) by Rama Educational Society, Kanpur. Its Founder Chairman was DR. B. S. KUSHWAH. Rama Medical College-Hospital & Research Centre, Kanpur is one of the leading Medical colleges for providing medical education in the State of Uttar Pradesh. The Campus is having clean air and water facility and 24 hours transportation facility i.e. Buses, Train and 21 KM away from KANPUR Airport. The Medical College is having more than 25 acres of land sprawling in a campus of more than 70 Acres of lush green land. The college is providing best Medical Education and training by conventional and modern methods including all the technologies. MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery) is running from the year 2008 and in addition to the MBBS course, Post Graduate Programmes in the subjects of (1) MD ANATOMY, (2) MD PHYSIOLOGY, (3) MD BIOCHEMISTRY, (4) MD PATHOLOGY, (5) MD MICROBIOLOGY, (6) MD PHARMACOLOGY, (7) MD COMMUNITY MEDICINE, (8) MD GENERAL MEDICINE, (9) MD RESPIRATORY MEDICINE, (10) MD DERMATOLOGY, VENEREOLOGY & LEPROSY, (11) MD PSYCHIATRY, (12) MD PAEDIATRICS, (13) MS GENERAL SURGERY, (14) MS ORTHOPAEDICS, (15) MS OPHTHALMOLOGY , (16) MS OTO-RHINO-LARYNGOLOGY, (17) MS OBSTETRICS & GYNAECOLOGY, (18) MD ANAESTHESIOLOGY and (19) MD RADIO-DIAGNOSIS are running. Our aim is to provided quality Medical Education to our students and, quality medical facilities to all.
